How much funding is being directed at each of the ten keywords from ‘Data 2020’?

  • For this question we chose to focus not on individual funders, but on the total amount of funding (and projects?) for each of the ten keywords/topics that make up our initial sketch of ‘data-related research’
    • As discussed in the methods section, for the sake of simplicity, we are defining ‘data-related research’ as a cluster of ten keywords drawn from the ten ‘hot topics’ in Data 2020 .
      • Data ethics
      • Data sharing
      • Misinformation
      • Data infrastructure
      • Digital economy
      • Value of data
      • Data rights
      • Data literacy
      • Digital trade
      • Automated decision making
